The iconic Reverso model finds its most classic and timeless expression in this Reverso Classic Small steel watch, whose proportions will grace even the most slender of wrists. Thanks to its case-turning system, the model can be personalized on its back face.
01
CURVED CASE
The lugs of the case are slightly rounded by the Manufacture's craftsmen to match the shape of the wrist and offer the greatest possible comfort to the wearer.
02
A SPACE FOR THE IMAGINATION
The watch's reverse face, revealed by a simple turn of the case, can be adorned with a secret engraved message or set with gems. Express your imagination.
03
A DIAL OF PURE SIMPLICITY
Clear and understated, the dial is the expression of a timeless style. Its background is worked with a delicate sunburst guilloché pattern and surrounded by a "railtrack" minute circle. Its Arabic numerals reinforce the pure, symmetrical lines of the piece.
